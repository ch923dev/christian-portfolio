How I work

Spec. Build. Verify.

The AI-accelerated loop behind every build. The speed comes from tooling; the correctness stays hand-owned.

01

Spec the contract

Data model, states, failure modes, and acceptance criteria, all written before any code. Most bugs die here, on paper.

02

Build with AI leverage

AI tooling generates the boilerplate at machine speed. Every architectural decision (schema, queue, state machine) stays mine. Days instead of weeks, and nothing outsourced but the typing.

03

Verify against reality

Synthetic events, Stripe test mode, redelivered webhooks, hostile edge cases. Nothing is "done" until the full loop runs end-to-end.
